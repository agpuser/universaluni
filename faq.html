Frequently asked questions

Are you curious about something? Do you have some kind of problem with our products?

Please feel free to contact us, our customer service center is working for you 24/7.

1. What to do if I have still not received the order?

Please contact the call center: +61 800 5244 0000.

  • Orders received before 12pm EST will be dispatched the next business day.
  • Orders received after 12pm EST will be dispatched the next business day after that. Please note this is a guide only.
  • You will be notified by email that your order has been received, following this tracking information for your parcel will be emailed to you at time of dispatch.

2. What are the postal rates?

For all Australian orders over $500 we offer free express shipping. We offer $20 Flat rate shipping for orders under $500.
